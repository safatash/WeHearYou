How far do you want to go?

WeHearYou already generates reviews. Here are three ways to turn it into a local SEO tool — pick the ambition level that fits your vision.

🌱

A — SEO-enhance the existing product

Keep WeHearYou as a review tool, but close the loop with Google. Add review response publishing (send replies to GBP), GBP post scheduling, and a review velocity dashboard.

  • ✅ Review reply publishing to Google
  • ✅ GBP post scheduler (offers, events, updates)
  • ✅ Review velocity & rating trend charts
  • ❌ No rank tracking
  • ❌ No competitor monitoring
  • ❌ No citation auditing

~4–6 weeks of build · Uses existing Google OAuth

🗺️

B — Full local SEO dashboard

Everything in A, plus rank tracking (where does the business appear in Google Maps for target keywords), competitor comparison, and a GBP health score for each location.

  • ✅ Everything in A
  • ✅ Local keyword rank tracker (Google Maps positions)
  • ✅ Competitor review & rating monitoring
  • ✅ GBP health score per location
  • ❌ No citation auditing

~3–4 months · Requires SerpApi or DataForSEO integration

🏆

C — Full SEO platform (BrightLocal competitor)

The full suite: everything in B, plus citation auditing (NAP consistency across 50+ directories), Q&A management on GBP, photo management, and a white-label reporting PDF.

  • ✅ Everything in B
  • ✅ Citation auditing (Moz/Yext data)
  • ✅ GBP Q&A management
  • ✅ Photo upload & scheduling
  • ✅ White-label PDF reports

6–12 months · Multiple third-party APIs
