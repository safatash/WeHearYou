Phase 1 — what ships first?

GBP Management has four distinct features. You can build all four, but which one is the most urgent — the one that would make a customer pay for this tomorrow?

A

Review reply publishing

Write or AI-draft a reply in WeHearYou, click Publish — it goes live on Google. You already have the draft UI; this closes the loop. The highest-leverage single feature for local SEO.

B

GBP post scheduler

Write offers, events, and news updates. Schedule them to go live on Google Business Profile automatically. Agencies love this — it's repeatable, brandable content their clients can see.

C

Photo management

Upload and schedule photos to GBP. See what's currently live on Google. Flag low-quality or outdated photos for removal. Frequently requested by brick-and-mortar businesses.

D

Q&A management

See all questions on your GBP listing, answer unanswered ones, and get alerts when new questions appear. Often neglected and a hidden SEO signal.
